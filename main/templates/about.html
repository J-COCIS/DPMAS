About Us

Welcome to the Disease Prediction and Medication Advice System (DPMAS)!.

Our platform is designed to assist individuals in predicting potential diseases based on reported symptoms and assisting the doctors in providing relevant mediction advice.

Healthcare Assistant

DPMAS aims to leverage supervised machine learning algorithms to analyze user-reported symptoms and predict possible diseases accurately. By providing early predictions, users can take proactive measures to seek medical advice from the proffesional doctors, potentially improving health outcomes.
